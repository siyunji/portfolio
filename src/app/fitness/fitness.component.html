UCI Fitness mobile application

Project Definition

The app targets UCI students who barely have any fitness knowledge, so we provide them free personalized professional advice based on studentsâ€™ individual physical conditions.

Problem

In 2016, a research carried out by US News and World Report showed that the University of California, Irvine (UCI) students are the top five percent university students who have the fewest sleeping time. Staying up until late night and sitting in front of their computers the whole day without sufficient exercise causes many health problems and worries. Many UCI students are willing to change their current situation. They want to improve their physical health but are hindered by the steep learning curve of professional exercising. Additionally, most college students cannot afford the price of hiring a gym trainer to provide personalized help. Worse even, similar applications on the market usually charge membership fees.
